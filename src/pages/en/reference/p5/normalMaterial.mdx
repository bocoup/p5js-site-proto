description: |
  <p>Sets the current material as a normal material.</p>
  <p>A normal material is not affected by light. It is often used as
  a placeholder material when debugging.</p>
  <p>Surfaces facing the X-axis become red, those facing the Y-axis
  become green, and those facing the Z-axis become blue.</p>
  <p>You can view more materials in this
  <a href="https://p5js.org/examples/3d-materials.html">example</a>.</p>
